The Peche In Mauritania

The fishing sector contributes significantly to Mauritania's economic development, through the substantial yields it provides, as well as its contribution to employment, broad economic balances and food security. And given that this sector is considered a source of renewable wealth, it has held an important position in the national economy and it is betting heavily that it will be one of the drivers of economic transformation in the next step. Mauritania's beach stretches from the Senegal River Delta to the white bay cape; its waterfront length is 720 km2, and the Islamic Republic of Mauritania has an exclusive economic zone estimated at 200 miles, covering an area of 234 thousand square kilometers2 , with its possession of a wide continental shelf of 39 thousand kilometers Square. The region has an abundance and diversity of its commercial-quality fisheries resources. permits, and they are currently under construction, and the amount of small surface fish processed in milling plants is estimated at more than 300,000 tonnes.
